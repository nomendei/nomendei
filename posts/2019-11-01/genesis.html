Genesis

First movement.

There we go, the first page, the beginning, time zero, the genesis. I decided to keep this blog post easy and simple, without going too deep into anything specific, just to make sure that at some point in the timespan of my existence I will actually finish it and publish it.

The picture on the top is the only decent result showing up searching for the word 'genesis' on unsplash. I would have liked something more biblical, but something astronomical is good as well. Biblical and astronomical... in the end, it just has to be about something bigger than ourselves.

And most importantly: about the origin of *this something*. Something that will give me a why even when I am completely bored and out of tasks. I will always be able to come here, derail my train of thoughts onto the keyboard and then into these pages hope that someone will find it useful, or not.

Let's close the first post of this blog like this, there is some text, some images, some science vs religion debate, some existentialism. And, how could I forget, a quote:

Those who have a 'why' to live, can bear with almost any 'how'.
Viktor E. Frankl, The Feeling of Meaninglessness
