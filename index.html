Introduction

I have been thinking a lot in the last years. Thoughts of all kinds, ranging throughout the whole spectrum of human knowledge.
I wanted a website just to write them down in a nice form and see if, once outside of my brain, they still make any sense.

They probably won't, but at least I've got a place where to let my mind wander, especially when I get bored after the 7th consequtive day of vacation at the seaside.

PS: seaside is fine, but I prefer the mountains.
